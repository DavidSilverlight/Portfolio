The curriculum

One campus, three lesson plans

Hosting University framed Microsoft's platform as a learning path. Service providers chose a track and followed it from fundamentals to advanced — Build, Manage, Scale.

🔧
Lesson Plan 01

Build

Developer & hosting tools — choose the Microsoft servers and dev tools for flexible, manageable, efficient environments.

🗂️
Lesson Plan 02

Manage

Server management — operate and maintain hosting infrastructure with confidence and control.

Cloud computing — grow from a single machine to multi-OS, cloud-scale deployments.
