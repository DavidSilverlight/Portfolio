Will you be the Unbeatable Geek?

A gloriously nerdy competition site — built in Silverlight — for staging the most ridiculous developer challenges at tech conferences.

🏆 The Unbeatable Banzuke📍 South Florida CodeCamp 2009🗓️ Feb 7, 2009 · Ft. Lauderdale
What is it?

Competitive coding, with a sense of humor

UnbeatableGeek.com gave developer conferences a playful main event: teams faced off in absurd, skill-and-silliness challenges. The site handled sign-ups, the challenge roster, winners and a rich Silverlight photo gallery — all wrapped in a bold, cartoon-fun brand. Click any screen to enlarge.

The challenges

Equal parts skill and absurdity

The actual events from South Florida CodeCamp — yes, these really happened.

Challenge #1
🍜🥤

Coding & Eating

Code something (add 2 numbers together) with one hand, while eating with the other. The code and the food must finish at the exact same time. The food of choice? Spam, Nerds and Red Bull.

Submitted by Zain Naboulsi

Challenge #2
🙈⌨️

Broken Arm Programming

One person codes blindfolded; the other gives directions while eating Saltine crackers — and may only describe what to do, never helping directly. Communication is everything.

Submitted by Daniel Egan & Todd Anglin

Suggested Challenges

⚡

CRUD Crunch

Build a CRUD screen in .NET in under 10 minutes. Bonus points for filtering and sorting.

🧱

Jenga Mountain

Move a Jenga set brick-by-brick from A to B — normal Jenga rules, 2-minute limit.

📱

Team Texting

Two people text a full document — one reading, one typing — against a steep clock.
