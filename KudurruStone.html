Refresh the experience, end to end

What the refresh delivered

More than a fresh coat of paint

The redesign didn't just modernize the visuals — it leveled up what crews can do in the field.

🏷️

A branded, clear home

A logo-forward dashboard that greets crews with "What would you like to do?" and three obvious actions — no guesswork.

🗺️

Built-in maps & routing

Tap through to job locations, get driving directions, and even see on-route conditions like flood warnings before heading out.

⏱️

Full time tracking

Start/stop timers, billable flags, hours, notes and a clean entries list with date-range totals — all redesigned for speed.
