The target device

Engineered for the DIAD 6

The spike targeted UPS's purpose-built DIAD 6 handheld — the device every driver carries on every stop. Proving .NET MAUI here meant proving it against real, rugged, high-volume field hardware.

What the spike proved

De-risking .NET MAUI for the driver app

A feasibility spike exists to answer hard questions early. This one answered the ones that mattered most for going all-in on .NET MAUI.
