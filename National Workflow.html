The interface

Built for people who move work, fast

Dense, color-coded grids surface exactly what needs attention now — overdue items in red, due-today in amber — while review queues, attachments and service-call lookups keep every order moving toward invoice. Click any screen to enlarge.

Inside the platform

A workflow engine, not just a list

Every order travels a defined path with hand-offs, sign-offs and a safety net for the ones that go sideways.

🔀

Stage-based lifecycle

Orders advance through seven clearly defined stages — each a focused queue with its own rules, owners and completion criteria.

⚠️

Exception management

Anything that breaks the happy path is flagged, routed and tracked until resolved — with full on/off exception history per order.

📊

Live queue visibility

Roll-up dashboards show open volume by customer and by stage, turning a 474-order backlog into an actionable picture.

🗓️

Daily & month-end runs

The same engine powers fast daily processing and heavy month-end closes without changing how the team works.
